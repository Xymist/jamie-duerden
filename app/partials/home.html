Hey there

I'm Jamie, as you may have guessed already. By day I pretend I know something about IT and communications systems; whenever I can arrange some free time I try to convert it into as many skills as possible. I think the general sense is something along the lines of 'jack of all trades, master of none': I'm less interested in being the best at something than in being usefully good at everything.

I have a blog, as you can see from the sidebar. This is part of a wider experiment in establishing a coherent online persona; dropping some of the many services and names I use in favour of my own name and a small set of necessary functions. I don't think I can abandon Facebook, due to the number of people with whom that is my only communication channel, but I've thrown aside G+, Twitter and Skype.

If you're the sort of person who cares what people read, I have a GoodReads page here; I only recently discovered the service, but it's already proving quite handy. My 'to read' shelf both physically and digitally is overstuffed.
